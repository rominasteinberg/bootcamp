Loki

About

This page is dedicated to the character Loki from the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

Loki Laufeyson is the God of Mischied, Evil and Lies. Is also know as the Thickster God.

He was the biological son of Laufey, King of the Frost Giants, who was abandoned and left to die shortly after his birth. Found by Odin, was taken to Asgard and raised by him and Frigga as an Asgardian prince, along with Thor.

Al recorrer el multiverso de marvel, se ha conocido que hay diferentes versiones de Loki en cada universo. Loki es desde un humano hasta un cocodrilo.
